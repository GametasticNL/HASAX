If you want to return your order, it must be in new condition with all labels, stickers and labels still attached.

After you have received a confirmation email of your return, it will take 2-5 working days until you receive your money back. If you have not received it within that time, please contact us via 7127@pierson.nl or 8428@pierson.nl
