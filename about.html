About HASAX

Caps aren't just a form of headwear. Caps tell stories. Stories about us. Our story started in Den Bosch. Living in the Netherlands, we noticed that caps and hats are becoming more and more about telling a story. A story about the person behind the cap or hat. With this in mind, we started HASAX. A cap and hat webshop where people can find caps and hats, which resemble their story. We try to find and design caps, which relate to the culture of the urban lifestyle. Of course we'd love to hear and see about your story. Caps. Because we all have a story to tell.
